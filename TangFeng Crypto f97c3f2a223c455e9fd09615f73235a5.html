Wallets

I use Metamask as my primary wallet, but have been looking to move to Rabby for its contract decoding and risk scanning so I know what is going on with each transaction. I have used Status, Argent, Loopring, and Math Wallet, but have my gripes with each that I will go into detail at another time.

For hardware, I believe Ledger has better coin support then Trezor, but honestly believe that a cheap android that you only use for crypto (almost air-gapped) with WalletConnect is close to my ideal mix of security and usability.

Defi Dashboard

If you use more then one protocol, I honestly think having a dashboard is a necessity. Having a total view of your financial situation helps put things into perspective. Zapper is my primary choice, as it has the best support for side-chains and rollups at the moment. A close second is DeBank, but their lack of rollups and light mode limits them for me. Zerion is third with their amazing chart of your money over time, but they have almost no support for side chains currently.
